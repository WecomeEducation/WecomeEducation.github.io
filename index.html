小学学术素养课程说明

At Wecome Education, our programme goes beyond homework support or short-term score improvement. We focus on long-term transferable academic skills.

Wecome Education 的小学学术素养课程，并非以完成作业或短期提分为目标，我们更关注孩子长期可迁移的学术能力发展。

Sentence Construction / 句式与段落构建
Logic over Memorisation / 逻辑重于死记硬背

Academic Reading / 理解学术文本

Systematically developing the ability to read academic texts with understanding.
系统培养学生深入理解学术性文本的能力，建立文本分析习惯。

Logical Thinking / 组织语言与思路

Organising ideas logically and expressing thinking clearly in writing.
清晰组织语言与思路，让观点呈现具备逻辑性与说服力。

Structured Writing / 规范学术表达

Scaffolded writing that teaches how to think, not what to memorise.
用规范英语表达观点。教师引导下逐步完成写作，而非套用死板模板。

"Learning outcomes present as gradual, cumulative progress in classroom participation and subject performance."

学习成果以“渐进式提升”呈现。随着积累，您会明显看到孩子在表达自信、逻辑清晰度以及校内学习表现上的持续进步。
